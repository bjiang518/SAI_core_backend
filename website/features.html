Homework Grader

Snap a Photo.
Get It Graded Instantly.

Point your camera at any homework page — math problems, essays, science worksheets — and StudyMates reads each question, checks the answers, and explains exactly what's right or wrong. No more waiting until the next day to find out you got it wrong.

  • ✅ Works across all subjects: math, English, science, history, and more
  • 💡 Clear, step-by-step explanations — not just a grade, but the why
  • 📄 Export results as a PDF to share with teachers or keep for review
  • ✍️ Pro Mode also evaluates handwriting quality with a score out of 10

AI Tutor

Ask Anything.
Get a Real Explanation.

The AI tutor feels like texting a brilliant friend who happens to know every subject. Ask a follow-up, request a simpler explanation, or go deeper — the conversation adapts to your child's grade level and learning style automatically.

  • 🧠 Adapts tone and depth to the student's grade level
  • 🖼️ Send photos — diagrams, textbook pages, whiteboard shots — all understood
  • 🎙️ Live Voice Mode: have a real spoken conversation with the AI, hands-free
  • 💾 Every conversation is saved and searchable in the Library

The AI Talks Back.

  • 🔊 Natural, real-time spoken conversation — no delays, no scripts
  • 🧑‍🎨 Expressive animated avatar reacts while it listens and speaks
  • ⏸️ Tap the avatar to pause anytime; resume exactly where you left off

Practice the Right Things.
Not Just Random Questions.

StudyMates watches what your child struggles with and generates targeted quiz questions automatically. No more generic worksheets — every practice session focuses on the concepts that need the most work.

  • 📝 Multiple-choice, true/false, and short-answer formats

Mistake Notebook

Every Mistake Remembered.
Nothing Slips Through.

Every wrong answer is automatically logged, analyzed by AI, and grouped by subject and concept. Students can see patterns in their errors at a glance — and retry the exact questions they missed until they truly master them.

  • 🗂️ Mistakes organized by subject — Math, English, Science, and more
  • 🔎 AI identifies the underlying concept behind each error
  • 🔄 One tap to retry a specific mistake as a fresh practice question
  • 📈 Watch the notebook shrink as mastery improves over time

Focus Timer

Study Hard.
Earn Tomatoes.

The built-in Pomodoro timer turns study sessions into a game. Complete a 25-minute session and earn a collectible tomato. Grow your garden, collect rare types, and trade up — because kids who want that Diamond tomato will actually sit down and study.

  • ⏱️ 25-minute focus sessions with optional background music
  • 🏆 13 collectible tomato types across 4 rarity tiers — Classic up to Diamond
  • 🌱 Physics-based garden where your tomatoes bounce around and stack up
  • 🔄 Collect 5 of one tier to exchange for a rarer one
  • 📅 Calendar view shows every study session history at a glance

Parent Reports

Stay in the Loop —
Without the Interrogation.

  • 🏫 Subject summaries written for parents, not teachers
  • 💪 Highlights strengths and flags concepts that need attention
  • 🔒 PIN-protected — parents control access; kids study without interruption

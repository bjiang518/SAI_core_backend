Accurate grader

The most accurate
homework grader around.

Snap a photo of any homework page — math, English, science, history — and StudyMates reads every question, marks every answer, and explains exactly what went wrong. Not a rough estimate, not "mostly right." Every mark is explained.

The reason it's so accurate: we don't lock into one model. StudyMates picks the best AI for each specific task — GPT, Gemini, Claude, ElevenLabs — whichever gives the most reliable result for that type of question. Grading maths problems and grading essay structure are different jobs, and we treat them that way.

  • 🎯 Works across all subjects and question types — multiple choice, short answer, essays
  • 🧠 Uses GPT, Gemini, or Claude depending on which gives the most accurate result for that task
  • 💡 Each wrong answer gets a plain-English breakdown of where the reasoning went off
  • ✍️ Handwriting mode scores neatness out of 10 — useful feedback that teachers rarely have time to give
  • 📄 Export any graded session as a PDF to keep or share
Learning archive

Everything your child has
ever studied, organised.

Every question asked, every answer given, every mistake made, every conversation — all saved and searchable. It's not just a history log. The archive is what makes the learning agent smart: the more your child uses StudyMates, the more it understands their gaps, their strengths, and how they think.

  • 📚 Stores every graded homework, practice question, conversation, and mistake in full detail
  • 👤 Builds a personalised learning profile over time — the agent uses this to know your child, not just generic students
  • 💡 The learning agent uses your history to suggest the best topics to study next — specific to where your child actually is
  • 🔍 Search across everything — find any question or explanation from any point in time
  • 🗂️ Organised by subject and date automatically — no manual filing needed
Agentic practice

Proactive. Personalised.
Built from your history.

Most apps wait for you to ask. StudyMates watches your learning history and steps in proactively — suggesting what to study next, building targeted practice questions, and flagging patterns before they become bigger problems. That's what "agentic" means: it acts, not just responds.

  • 🤖 Suggests what to study next based on recent activity and gaps — without being asked
  • 💬 After grading, ask the AI agent directly for more explanation on any question — no switching screens
  • 🌐 Curious about any topic? Ask freely — then generate practice questions straight from that conversation
  • 🔍 Practice questions come from your actual learning history, not a generic topic list
  • 📝 Multiple-choice, true/false, and short-answer — mixed up to avoid pattern memorisation
  • 🔁 Stop a session mid-way and pick up exactly where you left off
Feynman mastery

Don't just review mistakes.
Actually understand them.

Richard Feynman's method for mastering anything: if you can't explain it simply, you don't really understand it yet. When something keeps going wrong, StudyMates walks through the underlying concept from scratch — in plain language, step by step — until your child can explain it back themselves.

  • 🔬 Identifies the concept behind each mistake, not just the question that was wrong
  • 🧠 Breaks down tricky ideas into the simplest possible terms — no assumed knowledge
  • 💬 The tutor asks your child to explain it back, then corrects gaps — that's the Feynman loop
  • 🔄 Keeps coming back to weak concepts until they're solid, not just attempted once
Live AI tutor

A tutor that talks back —
for every situation.

Live mode isn't just a voice chat. StudyMates comes with pre-configured scenarios built for different kinds of practice — so whether your child needs to rehearse a presentation, sharpen an argument, or just think through a concept out loud, there's a mode for it. Real-time, interactive, and genuinely fun.

  • 🎤 Public speaking — practise speeches and get feedback on pacing, clarity, and confidence
  • 🎭 Presentation mode — walk through slides or ideas out loud; the tutor plays the audience and asks questions
  • ⚖️ Argument & debate — the tutor takes the opposite side, pushes back, and helps sharpen the reasoning
  • 🎵 Vocal compositions — practise poetry recitation, lyrics, or oral language with real-time feedback on expression
  • 🔊 Natural, real-time conversation — no delays, no awkward pauses
  • ⏸️ Pause anytime and pick up exactly where you left off
Progress

A learning curve you
can actually see.

Progress is tracked week by week, subject by subject, as your child studies. No manual input, no self-reporting — it fills in automatically. Most students and parents notice a measurable improvement within the first few weeks of regular use.

  • 📈 Accuracy trends by week and month — clear before/after comparisons
  • 🗂️ Subject-by-subject breakdown: see which areas are moving and which need more time
  • 🔥 Study streaks and total effort tracked — consistency matters as much as scores
  • ⭐ Points that reward showing up regularly, not just getting everything right
Reports

Know more than just
the grade.

Weekly and monthly reports give parents a fuller picture — not just what subjects their child studied, but how long they focused, how consistent they've been, which topics keep coming up as struggles, and any patterns worth paying attention to. Written to be read in two minutes, not filed away unread.

  • 📋 Weekly and monthly reports, sent automatically — no setup
  • 📖 Covers study activity, focus patterns, and general learning wellbeing
  • 💪 Highlights what's going well, and flags what needs a conversation
  • 🔒 PIN-protected — for parents, not for the student to stumble into

Privacy

Your child's learning history
belongs to your family.

Most apps treat your data as a product. We don't. Conversations are end-to-end encrypted. We collect only what's needed to run the app. We don't sell data, build advertising profiles, or share anything beyond what's in our Privacy Policy — which we've written to actually be readable.

  • 🔒 All conversations encrypted end-to-end — we can't read them, and neither can anyone else
  • 🚫 No advertising. No data selling. No behaviour profiling.
  • 📱 Learning history and mistake logs stored locally on-device first
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 COPPA compliant — extra protections for students under 13
  • 🗑️ Delete your account and all associated data at any time, immediately
Focus timer

Study for 25 minutes.
Earn a tomato.

It sounds simple, but it works. Complete a 25-minute session and earn a collectible tomato. There are 13 types across 4 rarity tiers — from Classic up to Diamond. Kids who are close to a rare one will actually sit down and focus, every time.

  • ⏱️ 25-minute sessions with optional background music
  • 🏆 13 collectible types across 4 rarity tiers — Classic up to Diamond
  • 🔄 Collect 5 of one tier and trade up for a rarer one
  • 📅 A calendar shows the full session history — good for building a visible habit

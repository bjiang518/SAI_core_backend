Built around how students actually learn

Not just a homework tool — a companion that grows smarter the longer your child uses it.

🎯

The most accurate grader

Snap a photo and get every question graded precisely — plus a full explanation of what went wrong. We pick the best model for each job: GPT, Gemini, Claude, or ElevenLabs, whichever gives the most accurate result for that task.

📚

Your full learning archive

Every question, answer, mistake, and conversation is saved and builds a personalised learning profile. The learning agent uses your history to suggest exactly what to study next — tailored to your child, not a generic student.

💬

Conversational AI tutor

After grading, jump straight into a conversation — ask for more explanation on any question. Or explore any topic freely, and generate practice questions right from your conversation.

🔬

Feynman-based mastery

When a concept keeps going wrong, StudyMates walks through it from first principles — the same method Nobel-winning physicist Richard Feynman used to actually understand things, not just memorise them.

📈

Progress you can see

A subject-by-subject learning curve that fills in as your child studies. Measurable improvement, tracked week by week — you'll notice it within a few sessions.

📋

Weekly & monthly reports

Parents get a short, readable summary of their child's activity, focus patterns, study habits, and general wellbeing — sent automatically, no setup needed.

🔒

Your privacy, protected

Conversations are end-to-end encrypted. We don't sell data or build advertising profiles. Your child's learning history belongs to your family — full stop.

How it works

A loop that builds on itself — the longer your child uses it, the better it gets at helping them.

1

Study and capture

Snap homework, ask questions, or talk through a concept out loud. Everything is saved to the learning archive.

2

Understand it properly

Get clear explanations — and if something keeps going wrong, the Feynman method kicks in to build real understanding from the ground up.

3

Get guided forward

StudyMates uses your learning history to suggest what to practise next — not random topics, but exactly what needs work.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data encrypted in database
  • Secure Authentication: JWT tokens, bcrypt password hashing
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access, role-based permissions
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews, dependency updates, vulnerability scanning
  • Secure Storage: Authentication tokens stored in iOS Keychain (never UserDefaults)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). For users under 13:

6.1 Verifiable Parental Consent

  • When a child under 13 attempts to register, we require a parent/guardian email
  • Parents receive an email with a 6-digit verification code
  • Account is activated only after parent verifies consent
  • We do not collect or store personal information until consent is obtained

6.2 Parental Rights

Parents of children under 13 have the right to:

  • Review: View all personal information collected from their child
  • Delete: Request deletion of their child's account and all associated data
  • Refuse: Refuse further collection or use of their child's information
  • Revoke: Withdraw consent at any time

6.3 Data Minimization for Children

  • We collect only information necessary for educational purposes
  • No behavioral advertising or profiling for children
  • No sharing of children's data with third parties (except service providers)

7. GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information in Section 12.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for the following periods:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active + 30 days after deletion request
  • Homework Images: Stored for 1 year or until you delete them (whichever comes first)
  • Chat History: Stored for 1 year or until you delete conversations
  • Progress Data: Retained while account is active for analytics
  • Crash Logs: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes
  • Anonymized Analytics: Retained indefinitely for service improvement

Account Deletion: When you delete your account, we permanently erase:

  • Personal information (name, email, date of birth)
  • All homework images and chat conversations
  • Progress data and study history
  • Authentication credentials

Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., to prevent fraud or comply with court orders).
